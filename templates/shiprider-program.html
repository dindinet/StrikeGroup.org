Navy Shipboard Multipurpose Copier Program

Strike Group provides Ship Riders who support equipment installation, maintenance, modification and technical training. Their role includes on-site technical support as well as escalation support for the customer’s trained personnel. The Ship Riders support the customer’s unique requirements and engineer custom solutions. Some of the many key points to making this a cost effective and successful program is the quality of personnel hired and services Strike Group provide to support the Navy customer. Strike Groups’ trained and experienced personnel are dispersed among customer locations on a global basis servicing ships docked or at sea.
